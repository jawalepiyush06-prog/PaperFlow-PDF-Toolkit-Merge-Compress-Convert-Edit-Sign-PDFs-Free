About PaperFlow

PaperFlow is a growing toolkit for working with PDFs entirely in your browser. No file ever leaves your device — every tool runs locally, so your documents stay private by default.

It started with one job: merging PDFs and letting you arrange the pages exactly how you want them, down to the individual page. That's still the core of the toolkit, and it's the foundation the rest of the tools are being built on.

What's next

  • Split a PDF into multiple files by page range
  • Compress large PDFs for easier sharing
  • Export pages as PNG or JPG images
  • Add text or logo watermarks
  • Password-protect a PDF

Why local-only

Processing files on your own device means there's no upload wait, no file-size gate, and nothing to clean up afterward. It also means PaperFlow works the same whether your documents are casual scans or sensitive paperwork.
